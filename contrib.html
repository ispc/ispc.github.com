Contributors

The Intel® SPMD Program Compiler (ispc) is currently being developed by Dmitry Babokin, Ilia Filippov, James Brodman, and Peng Tu at Intel. It was started with by Matt Pharr, Ingo Wald, and Jean-Luc Duprat. It draws from a number of language features pioneered in Ingo Wald's IVL compiler as well as parsing code written by Geoff Berry and Tim Foley.

Thanks also to the additional contributors: Alex Reece, Andreas Wendleder, Ben Harper, Daniel Schubert, Gabe Weisz, John Poole, Lu Guanqun, Mark Lacey, Michael Liao, Nipunn Koorapati, Pete Couperus, and Pierre-Antoine Lacaze!

ispc is developed "in the open" on github; contributions and improvements are always welcome.
